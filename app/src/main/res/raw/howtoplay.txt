To begin the game just tap the screen! Slide your finger left to right across the screen to move your paddle. Points are awarded to you when your ball hits a brick, and awarded to your opponent when you miss a ball. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins! After the game, if you choose Play Again, the game will keep track of the number of games won per round. If you wish to pause in game just tap the back button and hit it again to return to the Main Menu.
